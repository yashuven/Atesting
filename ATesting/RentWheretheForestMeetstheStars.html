Where the Forest Meets the Stars

RM 20

Rent duration (Days)

Product Details

Jo Teale, a reclusive ornithologist grieving the loss of her mother to cancer, finds herself on an isolated cabin retreat in the woods. Her purpose is to study nesting birds, but her real motivation is to escape her own emotional wounds. However, her solitude is disrupted when an enigmatic and unconventional young girl named Ursa appears on her doorstep one evening. Ursa claims to be an alien on a mission to witness five miracles, and she believes Jo is the key to making these miracles happen. Despite Jo's skepticism, she finds herself drawn into Ursa's world, and together with Gabriel, their reclusive neighbor who has his own painful past, they form an unlikely makeshift family. As the trio navigates the complexities of grief, love, and human connection, they are confronted with profound questions about life, death, and the nature of miracles. "Where the Forest Meets the Stars" is a poignant exploration of the resilience of the human spirit, the healing power of nature, and the transformative force of unexpected friendships.
